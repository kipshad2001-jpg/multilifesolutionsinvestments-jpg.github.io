Last Updated: March 2026

Multilife Solutions is designed to provide sports updates with minimal data collection. We may collect:

Our infrastructure uses background services to provide real-time goal alerts. This process checks for score updates at regular intervals. No personal browsing data or location data is tracked during this process.

We use the information we collect to maintain our platform, improve user experience, and send push notifications for match events (goals, start/end).

4. Third-Party Services

We use reliable sports data APIs. These providers do not have access to your personal identity. We utilize secure notification delivery standards.

5. Security

We value your trust and strive to use commercially acceptable means of protecting your information.
